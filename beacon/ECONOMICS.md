## Sustainability: Can Beacon Survive on 5%?

**Yes.** Here's how:

### Cost Structure
- **Infrastructure**: Netlify + Supabase (~$200-500/mo at scale)
- **Email**: AWS SES ($0.10 per 1,000 emails)
- **Moderation**: Part-time team (~$2,000/mo)
- **Support**: Automated + human ($1,000/mo)
- **Development**: Open source + core team

### Revenue at Scale
With 2,000 active publications averaging $50/mo each:
- Writer revenue: $100,000/mo ($1.2M/year)
- Beacon's 5%: **$5,000/mo ($60k/year)**
- Operating costs: ~$4,000/mo
- Margin: Sustainable with room to grow

### Growth Strategy
1. **Quality over quantity**: Better moderation, better platform
2. **Word of mouth**: "Save $5k/year" is compelling
3. **Values-driven**: Writers who left Substack over nazis
4. **Open source**: Community contributions reduce costs

## Why Substack Charges 10%

Substack's costs include:
- Infrastructure (similar to ours)
- Email delivery (similar to ours)
- Support team (similar to ours)
- **Platform advances** to high-profile writers (millions in some cases)
- **Marketing and growth** initiatives
- **Higher profit margins**

We don't offer advances. We don't need 10%. Simple as that.
